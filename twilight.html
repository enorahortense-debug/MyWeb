Twilight

Published: 2005

Twilight tells the story of Bella Swan, a teenage girl who moves from Phoenix to the rainy town of Forks, Washington, to live with her father. There, she meets Edward Cullen, a mysterious and handsome student who turns out to be a vampire. Despite the danger, Bella and Edward fall deeply in love, navigating the challenges of a human-vampire relationship. As their romance grows, Bella discovers the secrets of Edward’s family and faces threats from other vampires, learning about loyalty, courage, and the complexities of love.
